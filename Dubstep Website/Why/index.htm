Drop
After the bass cuts out momentarily leaving you hanging on the edge of the last note and some
swishy airy effects, the bass comes charging back in full force with as much or (hopefully) more
energy as were it was left off. Usually the crowd simultaneously knocks their head forward and
buckles their knees in pseudo drop style. When done in sync with the whole crowd, feelings of
group euphoria have been known to occur sans drugs.
Bass
The loud thump and bum sounds that resonate in your chest and make you "feel" the music.
